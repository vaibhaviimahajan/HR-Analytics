# HR-Analytics
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing employee attrition, job roles, salary distribution, and HR KPIs.


🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes HR data to identify patterns in employee attrition and workforce distribution. The dashboard provides insights into key factors such as salary, age, education, and job roles that influence employee turnover.


🎯 Objective
Analyze employee attrition and its key drivers
Identify trends across departments and job roles
Monitor important HR KPIs for decision-making


📁 Dataset
Employee dataset with ~1,470 records
Key features include:
Age
Salary
Job Role
Education
Attrition
Years at Company


📊 Dashboard Features

KPI cards:
Total Employees: 1.47K
Attrition Count: 237
Attrition Rate: 16.1%
Average Salary & Age

Attrition analysis by:
Education
Age group
Salary slab
Years at company
Job role
Department filter (HR, R&D, Sales)


📈 Key Insights
Employees earning below 5K salary show the highest attrition
Attrition is highest in the 26–35 age group
Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives have the highest turnover
Most employees leave within the early years of joining
Attrition varies significantly across education levels
